About me

I'm Vedant and I'm a Statistics Major

Over the past summer, I interned as a product manager at a road safety dash-cam startup NetraDyne. I was tasked by the director of customer success to modify the current distracted driver alert to improve its effectiveness. I oversaw this project from start to finish, from designing the algorithm that measured the effectiveness of the alert to carrying out the A/B testing of the team's suggestions to finally rolling out the successful suggestions to the newest release. I believe growing up in India, a multicultural and multilingual country has had a significant impact on my work. For instance, one of the suggestions I provided during my project at Netradyne was to configure the alert to be played in the language of the driver's choice, since similar to India, the United States is a melting pot of cultures and all fleet drivers may not be fluent in English.
